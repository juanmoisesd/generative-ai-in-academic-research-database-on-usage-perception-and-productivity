### New Open Dataset Published: Structured database on the use of generative artificial intelligence (GAI) in academic research and university teaching in Latin America (2022-2025). Includes data on: frequency of use by students and researchers, areas of application (writing, data 

**Universidad Internacional de La Rioja (UNIR)** — Juan Moisés de la Serna Tuya (ORCID: https://orcid.org/0000-0002-8401-8018) has published a new open dataset: *Structured database on the use of generative artificial intelligence (GAI) in academic research and university teaching in Latin America (2022-2025). Includes data on: frequency of use by students and researchers, areas of application (writing, data *.

The dataset is freely available under the **CC0 1.0 Universal** license, allowing unrestricted use, distribution, and reproduction.
