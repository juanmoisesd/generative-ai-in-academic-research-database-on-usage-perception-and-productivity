## Overview
Structured database on the use of generative artificial intelligence (GAI) in academic research and university teaching in Latin America (2022-2025). Includes data on: frequency of use by students and researchers, areas of application (writing, data analysis, programming), perception of reliability and ethics, and impact on scientific productivity. Data collected from regional studies, institution
